GALWAY - ENVIORMENT FRIENDLY CITY




♲ We care about the environment.

We create Galway 2020 together. Take care of a clean environment with us. Clean up after yourself, persuade others, join our activities. Cleaning up the city is a huge cost for us - help us - reduce the amount of rubbish, clean up after yourself.







Clean City, Clean Enviorment

Do you want to join us? We've prepared 10 rules that will make it easy for you to take care of Galway 2020 with us.
Get a trash bag.
Clean up after yourself.
Take waste to one of the recycling points.
At the food stands, throw the remains of the meal into the baskets, do not leave them on the tables.
Do not throw empty cans, bottles anywhere. Don't see the bin anywhere? Put in the backpack, garbage bags - you will throw everything away later.
Save water - turn off the tap.
Bring your own cups.

What to do

Recycler in Galway 2020
It will be possible to drop a plastic bottle or can into a special device that will be sorted. Everyone who uses the recyclate will receive a gift under the loyalty ecoprogram.
Bicycle parking
you will be able to leave your bike and - calm about your two-wheeler - set off into the city. And next to the parking lot there will be an outdoor bicycle service. Bicycles in the parking lot can stay even at night.
Artists will be waiting for you
Artists, musicians, WWF members and a healthy lifestyle ambassador will talk to festival participants about ecology.
Street Art Performance
A well-known street art performer who will lead eco graffiti workshops and create a festival painting will come to us!
